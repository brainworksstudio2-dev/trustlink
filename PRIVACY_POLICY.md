# Privacy Policy for TrustLink

**Effective Date:** June 12, 2026

At TrustLink, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the TrustLink mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
* **Account Information:** When you sign up for an account, we collect your full name, email address, and password.
* **Worker Profile Information:** If you register as a professional/worker, we collect details about your profession, experience, biography, hourly rates, availability, profile photo, and social links (LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, Portfolio).

### B. Device & Permissions Data
* **Location Information:** TrustLink requires access to your location data (both background and foreground) to display nearby workers or match workers with clients in their service radius.
* **Camera & Photo Library:** We request camera and gallery permissions to allow you to capture and upload a professional profile photo.
* **Internet access:** Required to sync database and chat messages with our secure servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
* **Match Clients & Workers:** We use location data to display available contractors on the local map.
* **Service Bookings & Status:** To manage requests, scheduling, chat communications, and progress logs.
* **Platform Security:** To secure logins, restrict unauthorized access, and protect transactions.

## 3. Data Storage & Sharing
* Your account details, location data, and profiles are securely stored using encrypted **Supabase** databases.
* Profile photos are stored and hosted securely on Cloudinary.
* We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data to third parties.

## 4. Your Rights & Choice
* You can update your profile photos, availability, rates, and location details anytime from the app's settings.
* You can revoke location permissions from your device settings, though this will disable nearby matchmaking features.

## 5. Contact Us
For any questions regarding this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us at: **support@trustlink.com**
